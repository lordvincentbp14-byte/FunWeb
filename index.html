Welcome to FunWeb

In FunWeb, the word “Web” captures three dimensions of learning:

  • It’s a Website that connects learners through technology.
  • A concept Web of relationships that mirrors how Physics concepts depend on one another.
  • The FunWeb (Fundamentals of Physics Web) is conceptually designed to resemble a real web, where each strand represents a fundamental Physics concept such as motion, force, or energy.

In a real web, the strength and stability of the entire structure depend on the integrity of every individual strand. If one strand is missing or weak, the entire web loses its balance and resilience.

What FUN Means in FunWeb

In FunWeb, “FUN” means more than just entertainment. It stands for:

  • Enjoyable
  • Fundamentals of Physics
  • Functional learning

Students don’t just memorize Physics; they explore it, connect it, and live it.

Conceptual Connections

Each Physics concept is interconnected; understanding one supports and reinforces comprehension of the others. When a student fails to grasp even a single concept, their overall understanding of Physics becomes fragile and incomplete. By visualizing knowledge as a web of relationships rather than isolated topics, FunWeb helps learners recognize the importance of conceptual connections, ensuring that their learning structure remains strong, coherent, and enduring.
