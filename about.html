FunWeb, short for Fundamentals of Physics Web, is an innovative learning and remedial platform designed to help Grade 9 students strengthen their understanding of Physics concepts through interactive visualization, guided exploration, and concept mapping.

Developed as part of an action research project by Bachelor of Secondary Education (Science major) students of Carlos Hilado Memorial State University, FunWeb transforms abstract Physics lessons into dynamic, connected, and meaningful learning experiences.

FunWeb is grounded in the Constructivist Learning Theory, which believes that students build their own understanding by connecting new knowledge to prior experiences. Here, learning happens through: Exploration: Students discover Physics principles through interactive activities and simulations. Visualization: Web diagrams and concept maps show real-time relationships between ideas. Reflection: Learners analyze and connect their experiences with scientific concepts. This makes FunWeb not just a study tool, but a thinking tool that helps learners reconstruct understanding through curiosity and inquiry.
